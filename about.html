Biography

David Edmund Papa (also known as Ethos), is an upcoming bay-area rapper/singer/musician. He also represents his hometown, Tracy, California, and the Asian-American community.

All mixing, mastering, and instrumentals are done by him. David is currently attending California State University East Bay studying art. He writes music about his experiences there and about love. David Papa Started off as a YouTube Artist playing acoustic covers.
